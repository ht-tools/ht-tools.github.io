Draining and refilling your hot tub is generally needed every 3-6 months, depending on usage. Here are the steps to drain and refill your hot tub:

Draining your Hot Tub

  1. Remove filters and place filter guard (and anything else that should be cleaned) in bottom of the tub.
  2. Add Ahh-Some or your preferred jet cleaner to hot tub and follow directions on cleaner.
  3. Shut off power to the hot tub at the breaker.
  4. Use a pump or built-in drain valve to drain all hot tub water.
  5. Use Off-the-Wall or similar cleaner to scrub inner hot tub surfaces, including inside of cover.
  6. Rinse hot tub and remove residual water and debris with wet vac.

Filling your Hot Tub

  1. Fill with garden hose to top of highest jet or manufacturer's fill line (insert hose in each filter inlet to start, to avoid airlock).
  2. While the tub is filling, spray the top of cover and outside of tub with 303 Protectant or similar.
  3. Once the tub is at the fill line, turn on breaker and check for airlock (follow manufacturer's instructions to break airlock if needed).
  4. Insert clean or new filters and filter guard.
  5. Test water with Taylor K-2005 or K-2006 kit. Enter results on Maintain tab and set Target amounts to determine chemical amounts needed.
  6. Adjust Total Alkalinity (TA) to 50 ppm using Sodium Bicarbonate (Up) or Sodium Bisulfate (Down).
  7. Adjust pH to 7.6 using Sodium Carbonate (Up) or Sodium Bisulfate (Down) - WAIT 2 HOURS AFTER TA ADJUSTMENT IF OPPOSITE UP/DOWN IS NEEDED.
  8. Add 50 ppm of Boric Acid to create buffer solution (helps lock in pH level) - use Setup tab to determine amount.
  9. Add Calcium Chloride to achieve Calcium Hardness (CH) of 150.
  10. Using Dichlor, schock to 50 ppm Free Chlorine (FC) to decontaminate (this will also put cyanuric acid (CYA) at ~40 ppm which is ideal) - IT MAY TAKE A WEEK OR LONGER FOR CHLORINE TO COME DOWN TO SAFE LEVEL (< 10 ppm). If you don't want to wait, and you are confident that your hot tub is contaminate-free, you can skip this step and add a regular chlorine dose via the Maintain tab.
  11. Once chlorine comes down to a safe level, switch to Maintain tab and begin using hot tub.
